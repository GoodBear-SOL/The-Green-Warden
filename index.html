Chapter I — The Legend Begins

He was not born.
He was chosen.

Long after the last banners fell and the forest went quiet, the old stories said the Emerald Flame had gone out — that whatever had once guarded these lands was gone for good.

They were wrong.

The Green Warden has awakened. 🌿

Chapter II — The Sacred Green Candle

One flame refused to die.

Long before kingdoms fell, long before the forest was silenced, there was a flame born from hope itself — the Sacred Green Candle.

It does not give power. It gives purpose. It does not choose the strongest. It chooses the purest. It burns once — but its light can last forever.

The flame remains. The Warden remains. The promise remains.

Chapter III — The Corruption

They were never born monsters.

Something changed them. Something turned their hearts crimson.

Where the red bears march, the ground remembers what they were before — allies, kin, guardians of the same forest, before the Crimson Curse took hold.

The Warden does not see enemies in them. He sees what was taken.

Chapter IV — The Warden's Oath

Not chosen to destroy.

"I was not chosen to destroy. I was chosen to remember. Every lost soul can still find the light."

This is the line that separates the Green Warden from every legend before him. He does not conquer the crimson horde. He redeems it.

Chapter V — First Purification

He does not kill. He heals.

The Warden places his paw upon a red bear, and the crimson curse begins to lift — thread by thread, the green returns.

Every redeemed soul strengthens the Emerald Flame.
